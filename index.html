Title: Dynamic Selection of Image Processing Filters for Convolutional Neural Networks
Author: *Yusei Kawai, Shinichi Shirakawa (Yokohama National University)
URL: http://www.jpnsec.org/symposium201802.html

Abstract: We consider adding the images transformed by the typical image processing filters such as an edge detection filter as the input images. Since the computational cost of almost the image processing filters is much smaller than that of the convolution operation in CNN, it might help to reduce the number of the convolution layers and lead to reduce the overall computational cost of the CNNs without the performance deterioration.
